Benefits of life coaching:

Life coaching can Increase self-awareness as this helps clients according to (lifecoachtraining, 2023) "gain a deeper understanding of themselves, their values, strengths, and areas for improvement."


Clients gain a benefit of more confidence in their decision making by working with a life coach "resulting in greater satisfaction for their life choices" (lifecoachtraining, 2023).


Life coaches provide support in setting and reaching meaningful goals for their clients to meet head on, resulting in fulfillment and accomplishment (lifecoachtraining, 2023).


Clients reported after life coaching experience to have a better sense of self-awareness and communication skills, experiencing better relationships with their firends, family and colleagues (lifecoachtraining, 2023).
